ROUTE

This tab finds all vehicles on an inputted UTA route (2,6, 72 etc). For Trax use 701 - Blue Line, 703 - Red Line and 704 - Green Line.

If the typed route number is valid, a map with pins denoting vehicles' position is displayed.

The map also displays the route shape in red. If you click the vehicle pins you can see the name of the route and the direction the vehicle is traveling. Also displayed is the vehicles' "on-time" status (colored dot)

Light Gray Colored Dot : Vehicle is > 10 min early

Blue Colored Dot : Vehicle is early

Green Colored Dot : Vehicle is < 5 min late

Orange Colored Dot : Vehicle is 5-10 min late

Red Colored Dot : Vehicle is > 10 min late

White Colored Dot : No info available

The selected pin also shows a blue arrow that brings up all the stops the bus makes (if the UTA api provides this information).

The map also shows a favorites button to add the displayed route to your favorites.

CLOSEST

This tab finds the 10 closest stops  to your current location and the routes/buses that stop there in the next 30 minutes.

You can also filter the results to show only the route your are interested in (type this in the filter by this route field).

If the typed route is valid, a map displaying closest stops that the route stops at are displayed on the map as pins.

Selecting a pin brings up the stop address (900E @ South Temple etc) and the distance to that stop from current location.

Selecting the blue arrow on the pin brings up the buses that stop at the selected stop (in the next 30 minutes), direction of the buses, arrival times and other stops the buses make.

FAVORITES

This tab displays the favorite routes added when you selected add to favorites in the route-show vehicles map.

Selecting a favorite brings up the route tab, searches for these vehicles and displays them on the map.
